About Me

I am an MSc student in Computer Science at Bilkent University, advised by Assoc. Prof. Aysegul Dundar. My research lies at the intersection of 3D reconstruction and controllable video generation, with emphasis on geometry-aware reconstruction models and controllable diffusion-based editing.
